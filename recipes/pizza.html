Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved classic that brings together a perfect balance of flavors and textures, starting with a warm, crispy crust as its base.
The dough, typically made from flour, yeast, water, and a touch of olive oil, is kneaded and left to rise before being stretched into a thin,
even layer. Once prepared, a layer of rich tomato sauce is spread across the crust, providing a tangy, savory foundation.
Fresh or shredded mozzarella cheese is generously sprinkled over the sauce, melting into a gooey, golden topping as it bakes.

Toppings can vary widely based on taste, ranging from traditional options like pepperoni and mushrooms to fresh vegetables, olives, and even pineapple for a sweet twist.
The pizza is then baked in a hot oven, allowing the crust to crisp up while the cheese melts and toppings cook to perfection.
The result is a deliciously satisfying dish with a balance of crispy, chewy, and savory elements that appeal to just about any palate.
Whether enjoyed as a simple Margherita or loaded with toppings, homemade pizza brings warmth and comfort to the table every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for the crust)
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the DoughIn a mixing bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit
    for about 5 minutes, until it becomes foamy. Gradually add flour and a pinch of salt,
    mixing until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes,
    until smooth and elastic.
  2. Let the Dough Rise:Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a cloth,
    and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  3. Preheat the Oven:Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone,
    place it in the oven to heat up as well.
  4. Shape the Dough:Once risen, punch down the dough and roll or stretch it into
    thin, even circle or rectangle on a lightly floured surface. Transfer it to a baking sheet
    or preheated pizza stone.
  5. Add Toppings:Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a
    small border for the crust. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top, and add
    fresh basil leaves or any other desired toppings.
  6. Bake the Pizza:Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes,
    or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve:Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice and enjoy!
