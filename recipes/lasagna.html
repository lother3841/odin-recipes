Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers rich flavors and textures, making it a beloved favorite at family gatherings and dinner parties.
To create the perfect lasagna, start by preparing a hearty meat sauce using ground beef or turkey, simmered with onions, garlic, and crushed tomatoes,
infused with herbs like basil and oregano. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles until they are al dente, ensuring they will hold up well when baked. For a creamy touch,
blend ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan, and a sprinkle of nutmeg to create a luscious filling that complements the savory meat sauce.

Assembling the lasagna is a delightful process. Begin with a layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, then spread the ricotta mixture on top,
and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a generous layer of mozzarella and a dusting of Parmesan for a golden, bubbly top.
Bake in a preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are slightly crispy. Allow the lasagna to rest for a few minutes before slicing; this helps the layers hold together.
Serve with a fresh salad and crusty bread for a comforting meal that will satisfy any crowd.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Meat
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Onion(chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce:In a skillet, cook chopped onions until translucent. Add ground meat and brown it. Stir in crushed tomatoes and season with herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Cook the Noodles:Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  4. Mix the Ricotta: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, an egg, salt, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional). Mix until smooth.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it Rest:Allow the lasagna to cool for about 10 minutes before slicing to let the layers set.

Enjoy your homade lasagna!
